Vegan Scalloped Pineapple

Description:

A sweet, tang packed punch of pineapple in a dense gooey casserole style dish.

Details:

  • Servings: 8-10
  • Prep Time: 10 Minutes
  • Bake Time: 1 Hour 30 Minutes

Ingredients:

  • 1/2 Cup Margarine
  • 1 Cup Sugar
  • 6 Tablespoons Just Egg, Well Beaten
  • 1/4 Teaspoon Vanilla Extract
  • 2 Cups Bread Cubes
  • 1 8-Ounce Can Crushed Pineapples
  • 1/8 Cup Plant-based Milk

Directions:

  1. Cream margarine and sugar.
  2. Add other ingredients and mix well.
  3. Place in casserole dish and bake at 325 degrees for 1 Hour 30 Minutes.
  4. Enjoy!
