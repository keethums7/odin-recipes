"Good Morning" Vegan Pancakes

Description:

Don't forget breakfast ever again with these tasty pancakes, filling and easy to make!

Details:

  • Amount: 16 - 20 Pancakes
  • Prep Time: ~10 Minutes
  • Cook Time: 3-5 Minutes

Ingredients:

  • 1/2 Cup Wheat Germ
  • 2 Teaspoons Baking Soda
  • 1/2 Teaspoons Salt
  • 1 Cup Whole Wheat Flour
  • 1 Cup White Flour
  • 1/3 Cup Brown Sugar
  • 3 Cups Vegan Buttermilk
  • 3 Tablespoons Just Egg
  • 2 Tablespoons Oil

Steps:

  1. In a large bowl, combine the dry ingredients - stir well.
  2. Combine vegan buttermilk, egg, and oil all at once with the dry ingredients.
  3. Cook on lightly greased griddle or frying pan, turning once when bubbles come to the surface.
  • Oh and, Enjoy!
