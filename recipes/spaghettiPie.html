Vegan Spaghetti Pie

Description:

This is a hardy explosion of protein, veggies, and pasta. Plan to wear a bib!

Details:

  • Servings: 6
  • Prep Time: 20 Minutes
  • Bake Time: 25 Minutes

Ingredients:

  • 6 Ounces Vermicelli
  • 1 Tablespoon Vegan Butter
  • 1/4 Cup Vegan Parmesan Cheese
  • 6 Tablespoons Just Egg, Well Beaten
  • 1 Pound Vegan Be'f Crumbles
  • 1/2 Cup Chopped Onion
  • 1/4 Cup Chopped Green Pepper
  • 1 8-Ounce Can Stewed Tomatoes, Undrained
  • 1 6-Ounce Can Tomato Paste
  • 3/4 Teaspoon Dried Oregano
  • 1/2 Teaspoon Garlic Salt
  • 1/4 Teaspoon Salt
  • 1 Cup Vegan Cottage Cheese
  • 1/2 Cup Vegan Shredded Mozzarella Cheese
  • 1 Teaspoon Chopped Fresh Parsley

Steps:

  1. Cook vermicelli for 6-10 minutes or as directed on package; drain.
  2. Stir butter, parmesan cheese and eggs into hot vermicelli.
  3. Shape the vermicelli into a crust in a buttered 10-inch pie plate; set aside
  4. In a skillet, cook ground be'f, onion, and green pepper until be'f is done and vegetables are tender; drain excess oil.
  5. Stir stewed tomatoes, tomato paste, oregano, garlic salt, and salt; heat through.
  6. Spread cottage cheese over vermicelli crust.
  7. Top with the ground be'f and veggies.
  8. Bake uncovered at 360 degrees Farenheit for 20 minutes.
  9. Sprinkle with Mozzarella cheese.
  10. Bake 5 minutes longer.
  11. Let stand 5 minutes before serving.
  12. Enjoy!
